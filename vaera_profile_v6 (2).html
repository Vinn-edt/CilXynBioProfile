Rules & Guidelines
✕

⚓ General Rules

  • Respect all members and the developer at all times.
  • No harassment, hate speech, or discriminatory behavior.
  • Do not spam messages or flood the chat.
  • Keep discussions relevant and constructive.

⚔ Service Rules

  • All service requests must go through Customer Service.
  • Provide clear and detailed information about your request.
  • Payment is required before work begins.
  • No refunds after work has started unless agreed upon.
  • Delivery time depends on project complexity.
  • Revisions are limited to what was originally agreed.

🗺 Map & Build Rules

  • Do not claim ownership of work made by Vaera.
  • Do not redistribute or resell Vaera's work without permission.
  • Credit must be given when sharing publicly.

💬 Communication Rules

  • Be patient — the developer may not respond immediately.
  • Use appropriate language in all communications.
  • Do not demand urgent delivery without prior arrangement.

Violation of these rules may result in service denial. Thank you for your cooperation. ✦
